Fiend Bosses List and Locations | Shin Megami Tensei 3: Nocturne (SMT Nocturne)

Matador

Matador is the first Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Foundation, which is needed in order to unlock the Amala Labyrinth and the 1st Kalpa Dungeon.

Matador can be found in the Great Underpass of Ginza, just after exiting the door guarded by the Manikin Gatekeeper (A). Once inside the tunnels, go past the frightened Manikin and prompts will appear stating that the menorah is acting up. If players choose to continue, this will trigger the Matador boss battle.

Daisoujou

Daisoujou is the second Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Eternity, which is needed in order to access the 2nd Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Players can encounter Daisoujou after speaking to Gozu-Tennoh for the first time. Daisoujou can be found in the eastern side of Ikebukuro, past the large room with Manikins. He will abduct the players in the hallway before reaching the escalators, marking the beginning of the boss battle.

Hell Biker

Hell Biker is the third Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Dignity, which needed in order to access the 2nd Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Hell Biker can be found on top of the Highway System, south of Ikebukuro. To access this location, players must go through the Guarded Door Exit (A) located in the 1F of Mantra HQ. Move south along the highway until the Menorah starts acting up. After a few prompts, players will be teleported to the Hell Biker boss battle.

White Rider

White Rider is the fourth Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Compassion, which is needed in order to access the 3rd Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

White Rider can be found once players reach Asakusa from the Ikebukuro Tunnel. Simply walk into Asakusa and a prompt will appear saying the Menorah is acting up. Keep moving until you reach the area in front of the Fountain of Life door and Amala Link Door. After answering a few prompts, players will be teleported to the White Rider boss battle.

Red Rider

Red Rider is the fifth Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Insight, which is needed in order to access the 3rd Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Red Rider can be found after using the Amala Link to travel to the Great Under pass of Ginza. He can be found in the hallway in front of the save room. After a few prompts, players will be telported to the Red Rider boss battle.

Black Rider

Red Rider is the sixth Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Wisdom, which is needed in order to access the 3rd Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Black Rider can be found in room B204 of the Annex of Shinjuku Medical Center. He only appears after you have defeated White Rider and Red Rider, and after you have completed the story mission in the Obelisk.

Dante

Should players explore the 3rd Kalpa immediately after beating Black Rider, Dante will be the seventh Fiend Boss they encounter. He will relinquish to the player the Menorah of Knowledge, which is needed in order to access the 4th Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Dante will show himself as early as the B1 Level of the 3rd Kalpa. However, he will only engage players in short fights before fleeing. The only way to fight him for real is to continue down to the B4 level where he will wait for the player before the ladder down to the 4th Kalpa Hall of Candles.

Pale Rider

Pale Rider is the eighth Fiend Boss players should encounter if they beat Dante first. He carries the Menorah of Majesty, which is needed in order to access the 4th Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Pale Rider can be fought after players hear Futomimi's prophecy in Mifunashiro. Warp back to Asakusa and talk to the Male Manikin in front of the save point. He'll tell you a new area is available. Head for the door that takes you to the B1 level of Asakusa and you'll encounter Pale Rider in the hallway just after the first flight of stairs. The usual prompts will appear about the Menorah acting up, then players will be teleported to a new boss battle.

Mother Harlot

Mother Harlot is the ninth Fiend Boss players encounter if they had previously beaten Dante and Pale Rider. She carries the Menorah of Beauty, which is needed in order to access the 4th Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala

Mother Harlot can be fought after players beat Sakahagi in the Yoyogi Park Construction Site. Simply enter the West Plaza and converse with the demons regarding a beautiful lady in the park. She can be found in the northern area of the West Plaza.

Trumpeter

Trumpeter is the tenth Fiend Boss players will encounter. He carries the Menorah of Glory, which is needed in order to access the 4th Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Trumpeter can be fought after players witness the birth of the Reason of Yosuga, in Mifunashiro. Simply warp back to the Ginza area and proceed to the Yurakucho Station tunnel. Trumpeter can be found after entering the station and turning left along the corridor.

Beelzebub

Beelzebub is one of the last few Fiend Bosses players will encounter. While he does not drop a Menorah, beating him triggers a world state that allows players to collect the Star Key, an item needed to unlock the 5th Kalpa Dungeon in the Labyrinth of Amala.

Fought in Hell's Hall in the Fourth Kalpa Beelzebub can be found in the depths of the Fourth Kalpa, inside the northern boss room in the Hell's Hall area. Players must travel through an entire Cursed Corridor area to get to him. To access Hell's Hall, enter the 12 Meters of Eternity corridor during a New Kagutsuchi phase.
